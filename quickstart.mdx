## Set Up Your Dashboard

In your onboarding email from us you will find a Custom Menu Link which you'll need for the next video on setting up your agency dashboard.

The Agency dashboard is where you'll gain access to all of our tools, systems and snapshots.

<iframe className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/pCTbsgzs7SQ?si=LTGjT5M3oW72wruT" title="YouTube video player" frameBorder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowFullScreen />

Where you can find your license key and the custom men link for setting up your agency dashboard.

1. Open your onboarding email and copy your custom menu link - this link can be opened on it's own, but for ease of use we'll add it as a custom menu link to your high level agency dashboard.

<img
  src="/images/download.png"
  alt="download.png"
  style={{ width:"60%" }}
/>

2. Go to your agency dashboard inside of high level - this is the first page you generally see when you log into your High Level account.
3. Click on Settings in the main menu on the left hand side of your page.

   <img
     src="/images/ClickDashbaordSettings.png"
     alt="image.png"
     title=""
     style={{ width:"30%" }}
   />
4. **Click on Custom Menu Links**

   <img
     src="/images/MenuCustomMenuLink.png"
     alt="Menu Custom Menu Link Pn"
     title="Menu Custom Menu Link Pn"
     style={{ width:"43%" }}
   />
   1. Create a new Custom Menu Link\\
   2. Select an Icon\\
   3. Give the link a title like - **GHL Power Tools**\\
   4. Paste your custom URL that you copied from your onboarding email \\
   5. Select show on Agency Dashboard, and turn off Account Sidebard, and show to all Accounts\\
   6. Select All Users for Role Permissions. \\
   7. Save.

   <img
     src="/images/CustomMenuLink.png"
     alt="image.png"
     title=""
     style={{ width:"100%" }}
   />

You'll now find you new custom menu link on the main menu of your agency dashboard in High Level
